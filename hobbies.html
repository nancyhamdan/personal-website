I started playing the piano in the summer of 2018. Although that was not my first ever experience with the piano or a musical instrument in general, as I’ve learnt the basics of playing classical guitar in 2016. Classical music is my favorite genre to listen to and play. However, sometimes I do play non-classical music.

Dans le noir is one of my favorite classical piano pieces that is composed by one of my favorite classical composers, Eric Christian. I often play Dans le noir when I want to relax myself or destress.

Mawtini is the national anthem of Iraq and is also considered as a non-official secondary national anthem by many Palestinians. I am a Palestinian myself and have always wanted to learn to play Mawtini on the piano.

Amour Interdit, or forbidden love in English, is another piece composed by Eric Christian. I find this piece very elegant and very fun to play. I particularly love how the piece intensifies as it progresses.

G Minor Bach is composed by Luo Ni and is a modification of Bach’s Prelude in C Minor. This piece is famous from the game piano tiles. The first time I’ve listened to this piece I was captivated right away and knew that I had to learn to play it.

Isabella’s Lullaby is an original soundtrack from the anime “The Promised Neverland”. This is one of the hardest things I’ve learnt to play on the piano so far as well as one of my favorites.
